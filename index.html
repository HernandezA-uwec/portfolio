About Me

My name is Abraham Hernandez, I am from Guanajuato, Mexico. Raised in Minnesota, I am a Integrated Strategic Communications with an emphasis on Advertising Major with a minor on Multimedia communications. Some of my passions I have is Writing, I spend a lot of my free time writing poems or lyrics for songs, although I don’t sing a lot, it’s still something I like to do a lot. But some of my goals I have is to be able to graduate college and be the first man in my family to do so. Once After college I’m hopeful to move to a big city such as New York or San Francisco and be able to work in the music industry.

Some of my experiences that I’ve gained are involved all over the place. I gained a lot of customer service experience working at Chick-Fil-A (2021-2023) and also as a Conference Assistant for UWEC (2024). Some other work experiences I have include being a Resident Assistant for Oak Ridge (2023-), plus I am also a TV-10 show Host (2024-), where I’ve hosted two shows, Gameshow which is our show where we bring people from all over campus to compete in different games like Jeopardy and Wing Feud. and Handful which is our variety show. I’ve also gained experience in Graphic Design as well as Photography throughout different classes I’ve taken in High School and College.
